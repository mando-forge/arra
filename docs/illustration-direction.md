# ARRA Illustration Direction

## Core Theme

ARRA's primary illustrations should follow a consistent two-founder journey. The recurring subjects are two close friends and co-founders in their 30s: one male and one female, both presented as calm, capable, equal partners. The founders should feel grounded in Manipur and oriented toward global-quality product work.

## Character Rules

- Use the same male/female founder pair across all major illustrations.
- Prefer rear-view or three-quarter rear-view compositions to preserve identity consistency.
- Keep styling practical and restrained: dark functional jackets, simple silhouettes, minimal accessories.
- Avoid romantic posing, fantasy armor, helmets, Viking symbols, corporate stock-photo behavior, and extra foreground characters.

## Visual Philosophy

The style should keep the current ARRA flat-vector cinematic language, but shift the mood toward Nordic philosophy: restraint, clarity, silence, craft, nature, trust, moderation, and long-view resilience. This should appear through composition, space, materials, and calm color rather than literal Nordic symbols.

## Palette And Setting

- Deep teal and blue-gray for the interface and clothing.
- Pine green, glacier blue, muted cream, and off-white for landscape and sky.
- Restrained cyan glows for intelligence paths, trust signals, and system connections.
- Sparse warm gold accents for human warmth and product maturity.
- Manipur hills, water, terrain, and clean modern architecture remain the grounding landscape.

## Section Beats

- Hero: the founders begin the journey, looking toward a luminous pathway and gateway.
- Vision: the founders map the terrain before building.
- Products: the founders overlook connected product districts.
- Learning/workflow: the founders walk through a humane learning campus.
- Trust: the founders review a transparent data vault in a quiet observatory.
